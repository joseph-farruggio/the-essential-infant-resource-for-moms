Is There a Car Seat Recall for
Your Infant Carrier?


Recall Alerts
in Every Issue
Still using the infant carrier from your last baby? Check this car seat recall list to ensure defects haven't been found and the car seat recalled.

Over 10 million car seats have been recalled in the past 10 years due to some manufacturing flaw.

Some involve serious flaws causing the seat to separate from the base in a collusion. Others involve a loose handle, flipping around and potentially dropping your baby on the ground.

These are serious recalls with serious results. Don't be caught unaware.

Besides requesting your free report below, subscribe to The Merry Mother and be notified every month of all the recalls that affect your baby.

Those items with a tag have been recalled within the last 6 months.

Side Note: Car seats and strollers usually go together like bread and butter. Check the recalled stroller list as well.
