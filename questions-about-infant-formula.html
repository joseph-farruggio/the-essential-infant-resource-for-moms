by Kayla
(Ollie, Iowa US)

We then tried the Sensitive for Fussiness and Gas, but the fussiness continued. When we took him to his 2 month appt our doctor recommended we try Soy for Fussiness and Gas. He has been on the Soy for 10 days and he's still very fussy and is now very gassy! His bowel movements are solid and he really stresses to get even the littlest bit out.
